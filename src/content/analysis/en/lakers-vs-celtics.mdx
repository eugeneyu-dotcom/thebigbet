The most classic rivalry in NBA history — the Lakers against the Celtics. On the two-way line, the home side is 1.90 and the away side 1.95, with the market barely separating them. That tells you this is a genuinely even contest.

## Lakers: Home Firepower, Hot Form

The Lakers have won four of their last five, with their offensive tempo clicking nicely, and the home crowd is their sixth man. As long as their outside shooting is falling, their transition game is very hard to contain.

## Celtics: Three-Point Shooting and Defensive Intensity Are the Lifeblood

Boston's system has always been built on elite three-point volume and switchable defense. Their problem is that once the shots stop dropping, the offense can stall — and playing on the road only magnifies that risk.
